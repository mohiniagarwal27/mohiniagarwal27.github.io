REGARDING POWER PLANTS

  • After analyzing a nuclear power plant we established that total thermodynamic losses exceeded 10% of rated capacity. It would have been possible to increase power generation by 6% of rated capacity, and/ or reduce fuel consumption by 10%. Much of these losses could be recovered.
  • We can study your boiler and steam cycle/ turbine operation. After we analyze your power plant the thermodynamic analysis will reveal where you are losing money by losing heat and power generation.
  • Our offer is limited to analysis and identification of losses. If you want us to suggest solutions, that will require further analysis and be another phase of activities.

METHODOLOGY OF WORKING

  • We will visit your installation and collect following information:
    1. Plant design data , original heat balance diagrams -- as the baseline data for comparison along with plant flow sheets / P&I diagrams
    2. Plant operating data will be collected and compiled
    3. Your compilation of known deficiencies (quantified) will be useful
    4. In case there are inconsistencies in the measurements, we will point out and request for rectification / recalibration of instruments
    5. We shall analyze and give you compilation of the results of analysis so that you can take corrective actions
    6. In case you want further analysis of the performance of available equipment, we can take up as additional work. This will help you to plan for the rectifications.
    7. We shall mobilize experts as needed e.g. certified energy managers, for boiler, coal transportation, control systems, analytical chemistry, steam cycle and turbines etc. They shall be scientists and engineers with many decades of actual plant experience in relevant fields.
    8. After the rectifications have been carried out then within three months we can repeat the analysis and quantify what has been achieved / what could not be achieved.
